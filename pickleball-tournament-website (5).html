    • Tournament Format

    Our premium format ensures maximum play time and competitive balance. Each player is guaranteed a minimum of 7 league matches, with champions playing up to 10 total matches throughout the day.

    Phase 1: League Stage

    12 teams divided into 2 groups of 6. Each team plays 7 matches (11 minutes each). Top 4 teams from each group advance to knockouts.

    Phase 2: Quarter-Finals

    Top 8 teams compete in single-elimination matches (20 minutes each). Winners advance to semi-finals.

    Phase 3: Semi-Finals & Finals

    Best of 3 sets format for semi-finals and championship matches. Winners receive medals and trophies.
