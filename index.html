    • Tournament Format

    Our premium format ensures maximum play time and competitive balance.

    Phase 1: League Stage

    Teams are split into groups and play their round robin matches. Top 4 teams from each group advance to knockouts.

    Phase 2: Quarter-Finals

    Top 8 teams compete in single-elimination matches (15 minutes each). Winners advance to semi-finals.

    Phase 3: Semi-Finals & Finals

    Winners and Runners receive trophies.
